    CLASS OF '21

    Life has been eventful.
    And here, The Class of 2021 presents our life, the small pleasures of the time we have spent at PIS woven into it. When we look back, at times, we become overwhelmed with the volume of things we have done. While you go through the site, eventually, you'll know the pleasure of having such a family-like school and memories from it. We have a lot to tell you.

    This website is not just a photo gallery but to share inspirational stories of every student in our class. We want everyone to feel how grateful we are for all the memories we have gotten as a gift from our school. It's to convey our message to everyone to keep walking ahead, as when you look back, even the valleys will look like small hills. The different stages of life bring with them a whole repertoire of emotions, ideas, and challenges. Also, regret is a thing of the present, goes away with time. Even guilt is fascinatingly powerful, and self-realization a must at all steps. We hope you can live the moments we have lived in and pace forward with happiness.
